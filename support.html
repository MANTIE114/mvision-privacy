MVision Technical Support

Last Updated: November 2025

Welcome to the technical support page for MVision — a local music player designed for Apple TV (tvOS) that connects to SMB network folders.

We’re here to help you set up, troubleshoot, and enjoy your local music playback experience.

1. App Overview

MVision is a tvOS application that allows you to:

  • Connect to SMB network shares within your home network
  • Browse and scan music folders
  • Play albums or songs directly on Apple TV
  • View lyrics and album art
  • Use playback features such as fast-forward, rewind, and repeat

2. Common Questions

  • Q: Does MVision require an internet connection?
    A: No. MVision works entirely offline within your local network.
  • Q: Where is my SMB login information stored?
    A: Credentials are stored securely in the Apple TV system keychain and are not shared or uploaded.
  • Q: My SMB connection fails. What should I check?
    A: Ensure your SMB server is accessible in the same local network and credentials are correct (username, password, and path).
  • Q: Why can’t I see lyrics or album art?
    A: Please make sure your music files include embedded metadata such as ID3 tags for lyrics and artwork.
